# LinkedIn Outreach Strategy — Omni Advertising

Omni Advertising is a mid-size agency, so a short, direct note to the right person can move your application to the top of the pile. Send your message the same day you apply.

## Who to target
Search LinkedIn for people at **Omni Advertising** with these titles:
1. **Creative Director** or **Post-Production Supervisor** — owns the editing hire and will judge your reel.
2. **Talent Acquisition / Recruiter** — routes applications and can flag yours.
3. **Executive Producer** or **Head of Content** — the team lead you would report to.

> Note: I can't browse LinkedIn from here, so I can't paste exact profile URLs. Search "Omni Advertising" on LinkedIn, filter by People, and match the titles above. Tell me the names you find and I'll tailor a message to each.

I'm a senior Adobe Premiere and After Effects editor with broadcast TV experience and AI-assisted workflows that cut production time in half. Happy to share my reel or answer anything: https://vimeo.com/1103241704.

**To an Executive Producer / team lead**
> Subject: Regional Automotive Video Editor
>
> Hi [Name], I applied for the editor role on your team. I've delivered high-end commercial and broadcast content on tight deadlines and would bring both speed and a strong story sense to your dealer campaigns. Reel: https://vimeo.com/1103241704. Would love to connect.

## Tone reminders
- Keep it to 3–4 sentences.
- One power statement (the 200+ videos / millions reached line).
- Lead with the reel link — for an editor, the reel is the pitch.
- Calm and direct. No hard sell.

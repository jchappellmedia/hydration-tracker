# LinkedIn Outreach Strategy — WhyGen Solar

Solar sales teams hire fast and value initiative — a direct message can get you a same-week interview. Search LinkedIn for **"WhyGen Solar"** and filter by People.

## Who to target
1. **Sales Manager / Team Lead** — usually the actual decision-maker for rep hires.
2. **Owner / Founder** — solar dealers are often founder-run; a confident note to the top works here.
3. **Recruiter / Office Manager** — whoever posts the job listings.

> I can't browse LinkedIn from this environment. Send me names and titles you find and I'll tailor each message.

## Message templates

**To a Sales Manager or Founder**
> Subject: Solar Sales Rep — Applied
>
> Hi [Name], I just applied for your Solar Sales Representative role. I ran my own media company for six years — generated my own leads, closed my own deals, and held 5.0-star reviews for over a year. I'm looking to bet on myself in a bigger arena and I'm coachable. When can we talk?

**To a Recruiter / Office Manager**
> Subject: Applied — Solar Sales Representative
>
> Hi [Name], I submitted my application for the Solar Sales Rep position today. Six years of running my own business taught me lead generation, follow-up, and closing — and I live in the Valley with reliable transportation. Happy to come to the Tempe office anytime this week.

## Tone reminders
- Match their energy: short, confident, action-oriented. These are not corporate readers.
- Offer availability ("come in this week") — sales floors respect speed.
- One proof point per message, no more.

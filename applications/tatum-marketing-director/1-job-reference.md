# Job Description Reference — Marketing Director, The Officer Tatum

## Application Details
- **Company:** Tatum Management Group LLC (The Officer Tatum LLC)
- **Role:** Marketing Director
- **Location:** Scottsdale, AZ — Remote
- **Type:** Full-time
- **Pay:** $75,000 – $100,000 per year + performance bonus up to 20%
- **Posted:** ~11 days before July 18, 2026 (via ZipRecruiter)
- **Benefits:** Health insurance, PTO, holidays, retirement plan, professional development allowance

## Company Info
The Officer Tatum LLC is a fast-growing media and e-commerce company. It runs a portfolio of brands including TatumStore.com, ShinergyLife.com, and PureChoiceApparel.com. The company partners with high-profile influencers and clients on campaigns, events, and content that reach millions of people each month. Its stated mission: combine innovation, performance-driven marketing, and cultural influence to inspire and engage communities nationwide.

## Plain-English Summary
They want a Marketing Director to own all marketing strategy and execution across their e-commerce brands, media presence, and client partnerships. The job is strictly marketing: multi-channel campaigns, a full email program, paid ads on Google/Meta/YouTube/TikTok, KPI dashboards, and brand consistency. They want someone who is both data-driven and creative, with 5+ years in marketing and 3+ in leadership.

## Key Responsibilities
- Develop and oversee multi-channel campaigns (email, paid ads, social, video, influencer collaborations) for TatumStore.com, ShinergyLife.com, PureChoiceApparel.com, and client brands.
- Build and manage a comprehensive email marketing program: segmentation, automation, performance optimization.
- Lead paid advertising across Google Ads, YouTube, Meta, TikTok, and emerging platforms.
- Collaborate with design, content, and video teams on materials for merch drops, interviews, podcasts, and live events.
- Drive brand awareness for The Officer Tatum's media presence (appearances, speaking engagements, partnerships).
- Support marketing strategy and execution for clients and influencers in the network.
- Establish and track KPIs, dashboards, and reporting frameworks.
- Conduct market and audience research.
- Oversee brand consistency across all campaigns.
- Manage external vendors, agencies, and freelancers.

## Qualifications
- 5+ years progressive marketing experience; 3+ years in a leadership role.
- Track record building and scaling campaigns for e-commerce, consumer brands, or digital media.
- Strong paid media expertise (Google Ads, Meta Ads, YouTube, TikTok).
- Success with email platforms (Klaviyo, Mailchimp, or similar).
- Creative campaign development for merch, events, or influencer content.
- Analytics tools: Google Analytics, Meta Business Manager, Looker, etc.
- Strong leadership, communication, and project management.
- Entrepreneurial and comfortable in a fast-paced environment.

## Compensation Notes
- Bonus up to 20% tied to ROI, customer growth, event success, and client satisfaction.
- Professional development allowance for courses, conferences, certifications.

# LinkedIn Outreach Strategy — Tatum Management Group

This is a small, founder-led company. Outreach matters more here than at a big firm — one good message to the right person can decide the hire.

## Who to target
Search LinkedIn for **"Tatum Management Group"** or **"The Officer Tatum"** and filter by People:
1. **Brandon Tatum** (The Officer Tatum) — founder. He may not read LinkedIn DMs himself, but a short, respectful note costs nothing. His public brand is faith, discipline, and country; keep the message plain and direct.
2. **COO / General Manager / Operations lead** — in founder-led companies this person often runs hiring. Look for titles like COO, Director of Operations, or Business Manager.
3. **Anyone with "Marketing" or "E-Commerce" in their title** at the company — a future peer can flag your application internally.

## Tone reminders
- 3–4 sentences. One power statement each.
- This founder's audience values directness — no corporate buzzwords, no flattery.
- Send within a day of applying.
